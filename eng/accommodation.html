Accommodation

  • The option to stay in the classrooms of Alatskivi School is included in the pass price. You will need to take your own mats and sleeping bags.
  • Possibility to tent on the school stadium with your own tent.
  • Accommodation options in Alatskivi: suites in Alatskivi Castle, Sepikoja Guest-house, about 4 km from Alatskivi is Nina Kordon Guest-house, Vaino Cottage, Nina's Straw Bale Cabins.

Catering

  • Catering in Alatskivi Castle (breakfast, lunch and dinner on Saturday and breakfast and lunch on Sunday) 14,5€ per person (if you choose this packet, please mark the option when you register)
  • You can also choose to eat in the Alatskivi Castle restaurant, in Kivi pub or in Sepikoja tavern
  • There is also a food-store in Alatskivi
