There is a possibility to choose between different types of passes:

  • Full 45€* / 55€ — includes 5 dance lessons, the swing dance party in Alatskivi castle, two nights in school classrooms
  • Saturday 35€ — includes 3 dance lessons during the day, the swing dance party in Alatskivi castle, one night in school classroom
