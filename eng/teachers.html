Ana Umnova & Tadas Vasiliauskas

Ana Umnova started dancing at the age of 16. After attending as many lindy hop classes and events as possible, she began teaching lindy hop and vernacular jazz in "Lindyhop.lt club" since 2011. She's also a student of Lithuanian University of Educational Sciences, where she studies the education of dance. Even though Ana dances totally different dance styles daily, lindy hop is an absolute number one for her. Tadas Vasiliauskas is one of the favorite social dancers in Vilnius. He's known for his unique footwork and great musicality. He also plays trombone daily and dances as much as possible. Ana and Tadas have been teaching lindy hop as a couple for three years and are known as passionate and inspiring teachers. They specialize in lindy hop, charleston, vernacular jazz and air steps. They are mainly focusing on dance technique and musicality, emphasizing naturalness and aesthetics of movement. They are performing all around Lithuania and even performed with Carling Family Band in Sweden. They are participating in dance festivals in foreign countries as students and more often as teachers. Also they won numerous of competitions during last years.
