If you arrive on Friday and would like to take part in the Onion Route tour, please mark the option when you register. The tour begins on the 31st of June at 15:00 from Alatskivi school and ends about 19:30. The tour includes: visiting Kostja's Onion Farm, Varnja Chapel, Voronja Gallery, Peipsimaa Visitor Centre and Alatskivi Castle (tour with castle maiden). The tour will be taken in cars. Price of the tour is 15€ and includes tickets to the castle and chapel, guided tour in Kostja's Farm and onion pie and tea.
