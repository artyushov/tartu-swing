Tartu Swing Summer Camp

Hello, all swing-dancers! You are welcome to Estonia to enjoy idyllic country life, explore the cultural Onion Route, find new friends and of course — dance! The Tartu Swing Summer Camp takes place on the 1st and 2nd of August. Swing dancers from Estonia, Latvia, Lithuania, Finland and all over the world are welcome to join!

Main topics in lindy hop classes are leading and following, some combinations. The classes are suitable for people who know at least the main steps and there are surely some new things for those who have been dancing lindy hop for many years.

We offer:

  • 1st and 2nd of August Dance lessons in Alatskivi school
  • The teachers are Tadas Vasiliauskas and Ana Umnova from Lithuania
  • Swing dance party on the 1st of August in the evening in Alatskivi Castle with Quintet of Cannons swing band
  • A nice holiday, possibility to take part in the events of Alatskivi Castle Days (takes place also on the 1st and 2nd of August), on the 31st of July — a tour of the Onion Route for people who arrive early
